- FastMCP's `CallToolResult.data` is the parsed structured return; `content` has the text representation. Both are redundant in prompts.
- Pydantic-settings `env_prefix` applies to field env vars, not to the `env_var` that `BaseLangGraph` uses to find the `.env` file.
